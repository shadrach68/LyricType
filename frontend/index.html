Typing Tutorial

Posture for Healthy Typing

  • Sit up straight: Keep your back straight and your shoulders back. Your upper arms should be close to your body.
  • Feet flat on the floor: Ensure your feet are flat on the floor or on a footrest.
  • Elbows at 90 degrees: Adjust your chair height so your elbows are at a 90-degree angle.
  • Wrists straight: Keep your wrists straight, not bent up, down, or to the sides.

Finger Placement (Home Row)

Place your fingers on the "home row" keys to begin. Your left-hand fingers go on A, S, D, F and your right-hand fingers on J, K, L, ;. Both thumbs should rest on the spacebar. This is your starting position for all typing.

How to Play

Objective

Type the lyrics of your favorite songs exactly as they appear. Aim for speed and accuracy to top the leaderboards!

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 5-minute time limit. Available to everyone.
  • Medium: 3-minute time limit. Unlocks after completing 5 Easy songs.
  • Hard: 2-minute time limit. Unlocks after completing 5 Medium songs. No backspace allowed on errors!

Controls & Rules

  • Typing: Type the highlighted word. Characters turn green if correct, red if incorrect.
  • Navigation: Press Spacebar to submit a word and move to the next.
  • Auto-Pause: The game automatically pauses after 10 seconds of inactivity.
  • Completion: Finish the song before the timer runs out to save your score.

Features

  • Training: Practice touch typing with guided lessons.
  • Custom Songs: Add your own lyrics to practice with any text.
  • Leaderboards: Compete with other players for the highest WPM on each song.
  • Genre Filter: Log in to filter songs by genres like Pop, R&B, Hip-Hop, Gospel, and Afrobeats.

About LyricType

LyricType is a unique typing practice platform designed to help you improve your typing speed and accuracy while enjoying your favorite song lyrics.


Key Features

  • Practice with real song lyrics
  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Detailed statistics (WPM, Accuracy)
  • Custom song support
  • Touch typing training modules
